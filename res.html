RESPONSIBLE TOURISM MISSION

RT Mission is the nodal agency formed by the Goverment of Kerala to spread and initiatives of Responsible Tourism alover the State.The Kerala Responsible Tourism Mission Society (KRTM Society) is the autonomous society working under Department of Tourism, Government of Kerala to spread and implement the ideologies and initiatives of Responsible Tourism all over the State. The Society has a Governing Body with the Tourism Minister as Chairman, Executive Committee, State and District level offices, and an autonomous authority.
