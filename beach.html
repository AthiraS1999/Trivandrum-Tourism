BEACHES

The beaches of Trivandrum are an immersive world of their own, with expansive stretches of golden sand and clear blue waters.Like varkala kovalam veli.You and your loved ones will surely enjoy spending time on these beautiful beaches near Trivandrum. The first is Kovalam, whose name is derived from “coconut trees”. There are three beaches at Kovalam, separated by outcrops jutting out to the sea. The second largest one is Hawah Beach. Its scenic beauty and serenity are supplemented by the availability of excellent accommodation. There are a lot of restaurants that provide grilled seafood items such as lobster, king prawns, and calamari caught fresh from the sea at Kovalam.
